    Justin Corr Joe Parris

Joe Parris is an award winning journalist who happens to absolutely love professional wrestling. Joe began watching wrestling from a very young age. Of course he was a major John Cena fan when he debuted on SmackDown years ago. It was The Rock and Stone Cold Steve Austin who caught Joe’s attention at a young age and kept him a fan for years to come. Over the years Joe has invested himself into wrestling from all corners of the planet.

When he realized WWE wasn’t the only wrestling promotion at the end of elementary school, his mind was blown. After seeing one episode of TNA on Spike TV Joe would never look back. His wrestling watching variety now includes promotions from Japan, Mexico, Canada, Germany, and the UK. His fandom evolved later in life as he became a referee for an independent wrestling promotion in Denver, Colorado. Joe refereed matches including the promotion's world championship match.

His refereeing eventually transitioned into commentating and ring announcing for the promotion. While studying journalism in college Joe applied his evolving knowledge of broadcasting to his favorite hobby, professional wrestling. If you ask Joe’s close friends and family, they will tell you he watches more wrestling than he probably should, but Joe will tell you it certainly isn’t enough. When Justin approached Joe about launching a professional wrestling podcast, the answer was immediately yes. Joe hopes to return to the ring one day with Justin Corr as his tag team partner.
